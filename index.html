Biography

I am currently a graduate student at University of California, Irvine working at visual perception and neuroimaging lab (VPNL) with Prof. Emily Grossman. My main research interest is to use multiple machine learning and statistical learning tools to understand the complicated and intricate brain networks for different cognitive functions such as action and event perception. I especially like the fact that we can combine multiple information in the environment to useful strategies and form concepts during daily interactions.

During spare time, I have a lot of interest. Recently I am becoming “tea connoisseur” and story writer. I am working on a dark comedy screenplay right now while also writing code in another window.
